John Marston

John Marston is the main protagonist in *Red Dead Redemption*. A former outlaw, he seeks redemption for his past crimes and tries to reunite with his family. He is a skilled gunman and has a strong moral compass.

  • Role: Protagonist
  • Background: Former outlaw turned family man.
  • Skills: Gunslinger, hunter, horse rider.

Arthur Morgan

Arthur Morgan is the main protagonist of *Red Dead Redemption 2*. A loyal member of the Van der Linde gang, Arthur is a complex character struggling with his morality, past, and relationships within the gang.

  • Role: Protagonist (Red Dead Redemption 2)
  • Background: Outlaw, former gang member.
  • Skills: Gunslinger, tracker, animal handler.

Dutch Van der Linde

Dutch Van der Linde is the charismatic leader of the Van der Linde gang. Once a father figure to Arthur and John, his increasing instability and ideological beliefs create conflict within the gang.

  • Role: Antagonist, Gang Leader
  • Background: Leader of the Van der Linde gang.
  • Skills: Strategist, persuasive speaker.

Sadie Adler

Sadie Adler is a strong, independent woman who becomes a vital member of the Van der Linde gang. Initially mourning the death of her husband, Sadie becomes a ruthless and skilled fighter.

  • Role: Gang Member
  • Background: Widow, former housewife turned outlaw.
  • Skills: Gunslinger, sharpshooter, fighter.
